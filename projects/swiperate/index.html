Features

Everything You Need to Rate Smarter

From simple image sorting to complex multi-reviewer content curation — SwipeRate handles it all.

📐

XY Spatial Rating

Drag to any position on a 2D grid to rate two properties simultaneously. 9 distinct zones for nuanced tagging.

🧊

XYZ Depth Rating

Add a third dimension with scroll/pinch gestures. Rate 3 properties at once without any extra steps.

👥

Multi-Reviewer

Multiple people or AI characters can rate the same content. Compare ratings, resolve conflicts, track attribution.

📎

Any File Type

Images, videos, audio, 3D models, and documents. Each gets a tailored preview with zero configuration.

💬

Annotations + Threads

Pin annotations, draw regions, add timestamped markers. Full threaded conversations with @mentions.

🔗

Backlinks + Sync

Files can exist in multiple places. Backlinking, forward linking, sync vs local mode, and revision history.

How it Works

Rate in Seconds, Not Minutes

SwipeRate turns content review from a tedious process into an instant spatial gesture.

Load Content

Import a deck of items — images, profiles, agents, products. Any file type works.

Configure Axes

Label X, Y, and Z axes with your rating properties. "Quality vs Priority", "Skill vs Creativity", etc.

Drag to Rate

Each card appears in the arena. Drag it to the zone that represents your rating. Scroll for depth.

Export Results

Full rating data, tags, tiers, reviewer attribution, and revision history — all exportable as JSON.
