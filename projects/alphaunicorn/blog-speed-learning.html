Learn How to Listen to Audiobooks & Video at 3x to 10x Speed

By Justin Massion • Alpha Unicorn

What if you could consume 10 books in the time it normally takes to finish one? Speed listening and watching is a superpower that separates top performers from everyone else.

Why Speed Matters

Knowledge compounds. The more you learn, the faster you can connect ideas, spot opportunities, and make decisions. Speed consumption is one of the most powerful productivity multipliers available to any entrepreneur.

Training Your Brain

Your brain is far more capable of processing information than you think. Start at 1.5x speed and gradually work up. Within weeks, 3x will feel normal. Some Alpha Unicorn practitioners regularly consume content at 5x–10x speed.

The Progression

  • Week 1-2: Start at 1.5x speed — get comfortable with faster audio
  • Week 3-4: Move to 2x — this becomes your new normal
  • Month 2: Push to 3x — you're now consuming 3x more content
  • Month 3+: Experiment with 5x-10x for familiar topics and review material

Tools & Techniques

Use apps that support variable playback speed. Combine speed listening with active note-taking and spaced repetition for maximum retention. The goal isn't just to hear more — it's to learn more.

Speed learning is a core pillar of the Alpha Unicorn 10x Productivity framework. It's not about rushing — it's about optimizing your most valuable resource: time.
