# 👤 Characters & Teams
People — real and virtual — who inhabit the AntiGravity ecosystem.

## Characters (Virtual)
AI agents and virtual personas with defined appearances and roles.
- Name, role, skills, personality
- Visual appearance (for 3D rendering)
- Workstation setup
- Active projects

## Real People
Contacts and collaborators mapped from [[08-crm]].

## Teams
Groups of characters working toward shared goals.
- Team name and mission
- Member roster
- Active projects
- Communication channels
