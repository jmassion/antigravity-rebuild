🧬 What is FranchiseOS?

FranchiseOS is AntiGravity's framework for transforming standalone business concepts into scalable, repeatable franchise operations. Each franchise gets its own identity, workflows, and AI-powered automation stack — while sharing the underlying infrastructure.

Think of it as a factory for businesses: define the concept, configure the template, and deploy new instances with consistent branding, operations, and growth systems.
