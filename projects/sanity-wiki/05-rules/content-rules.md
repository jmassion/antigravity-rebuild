# ✍️ Content Rules

Standards for wiki content, documentation, and published material.

## Formatting
- Use markdown for all content
- Follow the page template in [[00-getting-started/05-conventions]]
- Use `[[wiki-links]]` for cross-references
- Include "See also" footer with related links
- Add last-updated dates

## Media
- Use visuals to communicate, not just decorate
- Hero images for major pages
- Diagrams for complex relationships
- Screenshots for UI documentation

## Tone
- Professional but approachable
- Clear and direct
- Use active voice
- Avoid jargon without explanation
