🎯 Concept Overview

Skool is a community-first learning platform that combines course delivery with community engagement. Unlike traditional LMS platforms, Skool prioritizes peer interaction, gamification, and group accountability to drive completion rates.

Through FranchiseOS, educators, coaches, and creators can launch their own branded Skool instance with custom curriculum templates, payment processing, certificate generation, and community management tools.
