# 🌍 Worlds & Themes
Overarching environments and aesthetic contexts for the AntiGravity ecosystem.

## What Are Worlds?
Worlds are the top-level containers for environments, characters, and objects. Each world has a distinct visual theme, set of rules, and atmosphere.

## Examples
| World | Theme | Description |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| **Professional Studio** | Clean, modern, corporate | Default workspace environment |
| **Cyberpunk Lab** | Neon, dark, futuristic | High-tech experimental space |
| **Clean Room** | White, minimal, focused | Empty white room for content visualization |

## Themes
Themes define: color palette, lighting, textures, mood, sound design (future).
