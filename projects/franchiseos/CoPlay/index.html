🎯 Concept Overview

CoPlay is a social gaming platform designed to make multiplayer experiences effortless. Whether it's competitive esports or casual party games, CoPlay handles matchmaking, party formation, and session management across devices.

Built as a FranchiseOS template, any gaming community, esports organization, or entertainment venue can deploy their own branded CoPlay instance with custom game integrations and community features.
