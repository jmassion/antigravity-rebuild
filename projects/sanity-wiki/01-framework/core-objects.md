# 🧩 Core Objects

The foundational object types that make up the AntiGravity "playset."

## Object Taxonomy

### 🌍 Worlds & Themes
Overarching environments and aesthetic contexts.
- Worlds contain environments, characters, and rules
- Themes define visual style, mood, and palette
- Examples: *Professional Studio*, *Cyberpunk Lab*, *Clean Room*

### 🪨 Materials & Objects
Physical and virtual items that populate worlds.
- Materials define textures, properties, behaviors
- Objects are instances with position, state, and history
- Decorations are cosmetic/environmental items

### 👤 Characters & Teams
People (real and virtual) who inhabit and interact with worlds.
- **Characters**: AI agents, virtual personas, avatars
- **Real People**: Contacts, collaborators, stakeholders
- **Teams**: Groups with shared goals and projects
- Each has: name, role, skills, appearance, workstation

### 🏢 Companies & Organizations
Business entities and organizational structures.
- Companies have people, projects, and relationships
- Organizations can be nested (departments, divisions)
- Mapped to [[08-crm]] for relationship tracking

### ⏰ Time, Tasks & Projects
Work management objects.
- **Time**: Schedules, deadlines, milestones
- **Tasks**: Individual work items with status
- **Projects**: Collections of tasks with goals
- **Processes**: Repeatable workflows

### 🏛️ Infrastructure
Physical and virtual workspaces.
- **Workstations**: Where work happens
- **Devices**: Hardware & peripherals
- **Studios**: Creative workspaces
- **Environments**: Virtual rooms/spaces

### 🔌 Connectors & Integrations
How systems talk to each other.
- API connections
- MCP servers
- File sync providers
- Marketplace integrations

### 🏷️ Tags & Taxonomy
How everything is categorized and found.
- Hierarchical tag system
- Auto-generated from content
- Powers search and filtering

### 💰 Financial
- **Bank Accounts**: Financial tracking
- **Transactions**: Money movement
- **Budgets**: Project/team budgets

### 📦 Apps & Tools
Software and services used across projects.
- Internal tools
- External services
- Marketplaces

### 🔐 Sharing & Permissions
Who can see and do what.
- Role-based access
- Project-level permissions
- Publishing permissions
