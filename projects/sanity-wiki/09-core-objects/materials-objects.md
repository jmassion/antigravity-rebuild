# 🪨 Materials & Objects
Physical and virtual items that populate worlds.

## Materials
Define textures, properties, and behaviors.
- Surface types: glass, metal, wood, fabric, holographic
- Physical properties: weight, transparency, reflectivity
- Interactive behaviors: clickable, draggable, animatable

## Objects & Decorations
- **Functional objects**: Screens, keyboards, tools
- **Decorative objects**: Plants, artwork, lighting
- **Props**: Role-specific items matching character function
