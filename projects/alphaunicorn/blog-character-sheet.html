Design the Perfect Character Sheet for Every Role in Your Business

By Justin Massion • Alpha Unicorn

What if you could design the perfect employee for every role in your company — with precisely defined skills, responsibilities, KPIs, and workflows? That's the power of the Character Sheet methodology.

The RPG Approach to Business

Inspired by role-playing game character sheets, this methodology treats every position as a character with defined attributes, skills, and capabilities. When you document exactly what success looks like for each role, you can optimize, automate, and scale with precision.

Building Your Character Sheet

  • Core Stats: Define the essential skills and competencies for the role
  • Daily Quests: Map out specific daily tasks and their expected outcomes
  • Skill Tree: Create growth paths and advancement criteria
  • Equipment: List all tools, software, and resources needed
  • Boss Metrics: Define the KPIs that determine success

Let AI Manage Your Company

Once every role is perfectly documented in a character sheet, you can begin automating with AI. The character sheet becomes the blueprint that AI agents follow to execute tasks, make decisions, and manage operations as perfect employees.

The future of business isn't about hiring more people — it's about designing perfect character sheets and letting intelligent systems execute them flawlessly.
