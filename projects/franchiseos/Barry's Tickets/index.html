🎯 Concept Overview

Barry's Tickets reimagines the event ticketing experience with AI-powered pricing and seat selection. The platform uses machine learning to predict demand, optimize pricing in real-time, and recommend the best seats based on individual preferences.

Built as a FranchiseOS template, Barry's Tickets can be white-labeled for any venue, promoter, or entertainment brand wanting to operate their own smart ticketing platform.
